PDF scan dang demo.

Checklist truoc khi import:
- Trang scan thang, ro, khong nghieng.
- Do phan giai nen tu 200-300 DPI tro len.
- Moi cau nen nam gon trong mot vung, tranh bo cuc nhieu cot.
- Neu co hinh hoac bang, can kiem tra lai thu cong sau OCR.
- Neu co file Word goc, nen import DOCX thay vi PDF scan.
